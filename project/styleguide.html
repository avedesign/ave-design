Bosch Styleguide

overview

A cross-divisional design guideline project created to bring greater consistency to Bosch product design.

The goal was to make Bosch products feel more consistent while still allowing flexibility for different markets and technologies. Regardless of what department of division the product came from, the end user should see Bosch as one.

 

As project lead, the work involved aligning many 30+ internal stakeholders as well as external agencies and turning strategic design principles into usable guidance for future product development.
