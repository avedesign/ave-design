Deepfield sensor

overview

Starting from a Robot to pivot to a service

Through in depth research, the project identified that farmers did not need autonomous robots as much as reliable field data to support daily decisions. By focusing on asparagus farming, a high-value crop where precise intervention matters, the solution delivered four critical temperature values remotely.

 

The product combined rugged farm-equipment qualities with a clear step toward digital agriculture. The product sold out shortly after launch. Deepfield became a successful new business segment within the Bosch group
