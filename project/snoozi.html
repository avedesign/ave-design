Stokke Snoozi

overview

A new product to enter the bassinet market. Lightweight, refined and blednign into any environment were key.

Stokke Snoozi marked Stokke's return to the bassinet category with a lightweight, minimalist design created to blend naturally into modern living spaces. Built from just four legs, an aluminium frame, a breathable textile liner and a mattress, the design focuses on simplicity, functionality and refined details but has a magical element. An integrated adjustment system allows the sleeping height to be changed without additional accessories.

 

I guided the design through engineering and industrialisation, while preserving the original design intent. The concept was created by Permafrost.
